REGULAR LOANS

Company's loan eligibility will prevail
  • A type of loan for personal, family and household use.
    • A new member can avail initial loan if he is at least three months as a member with corresponding loanable amount as follows:
      • Initial loan of PhP1, 000.00 to be deducted for a maximum period of one (1) month
      • Second loan of PhP2, 000.00 to be deducted for a maximum period of two (2) months
      • Third loan of PhP3, 000.00 to be deducted for a maximum period of three (3) months
      • Maximum of 80% of the total paid up share for the succeeding loan
    • Applicant must meet the required minimum take-home pay of 20% of gross pay per cut-off.
    • Applicant agrees to pay the applicable interest rate based on his / her chosen payment duration.
    • Payment Terms and Interest Rate:
      • 1-3 months - 1.25% per month
      • 4 months - 1.50% per month
      • 5 months - 1.75% per month
      • 6 months - 2% per month
    • Coop loans proceeds will be net of the above interest.
    • Monthly loan payment amount is equal to loan amount divided by number of months to pay. Amount of deduction per pay day is equivalent to 1/2 of monthly loan payment.
    • A regular member can only renew his/her loan if he/she has paid 50% of his/her existing loan.
    • For an associate member, loan can be renewed if he/she has a remaining three (3) loan deductions.

EDUCATIONAL LOANS

  • Are loans for the purpose of Calamity (fortuitous events), Disasters and Hospitalization.
  • Loans should pass through all the BODs and requires at least three signatures.
  • Maximum loanable amount is PhP30, 000.00 regardless of Regular loan balance but still with the discretion of credit committee if salary is sufficient for deduction with 1.75% Interest per month
  • Payable within five (5) months with add on interest principle
  • Requirements to avail this loan are as follows:
    • Assessment from School / School supplies.
    • If married or Single parent – loan applied should be for children
    • If single – loan applied should be for brother/sister

EMERGENCY LOANS

  • A loan for coop member who has children, brothers and sisters that goes to school.
  • Hospitalization:
    • Maximum loanable amount is PhP30, 000.00
    • Interest rate is 1% per month maximum of 1 year to pay with add on interest principle
    • Only the immediate families are entitled to avail this particular loan
    • Immediate families are parents, spouse, children, brothers, and sisters
    • Requirement will be medical certificate or any hospital documents that will prove of patient’s hospitalization
    • If loan has been approved, the member is only allowed to apply for another loan provided that his/her total amount of loan is not more than his/her total capital share and the requirement of 20% take home pay must be met
  • Calamity:
    • Maximum loanable amount is PhP30, 000.00
    • Interest rate is 1% per month maximum of 1 year to pay with add on interest principle
    • Requirements to avail this loan are barangay clearance and pictures of damaged property
    • If loan has been approved, the member is only allowed to apply for another loan provided that his/her total amount of loan is not more than his/her total capital share and the requirement of 20% take home pay must be met
